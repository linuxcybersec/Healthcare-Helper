Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Healthcare Helper

Is my health data secure and private?
Yes! All your health data is processed locally in your browser and never sent to our servers. We prioritize your privacy and security above all else.
How accurate is the diabetes risk assessment?
Our assessment is based on established medical research and risk factors. However, it's not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult with healthcare professionals.
Is Healthcare Helper free to use?
Yes! Healthcare Helper is completely free to use. We believe that health assessment tools should be accessible to everyone.
Can I contribute to the project?
Absolutely! This is an open-source project built for Hackathon 2025. Check out our GitHub repository to contribute code, report bugs, or suggest features.
What should I do if I get a high-risk result?
If you receive a high-risk assessment, we recommend consulting with a healthcare professional immediately. Our tool is for awareness and should not replace medical consultation.
