About Healthcare Helper

Empowering individuals with personalized diabetes risk assessment tools built for Hackathon 2025

Built for Zero Boundaries Hackathon 2025

This innovative project was created to address the growing global issue of diabetes awareness and prevention. Our mission is to make diabetes risk assessment accessible, accurate, and actionable for everyone.

Our Mission & Vision

Transforming healthcare through technology and accessibility

Our Mission

To democratize healthcare by providing accessible, accurate, and personalized diabetes risk assessment tools that empower individuals to take control of their health journey. We believe prevention is better than cure, and this app empowers individuals to take action before it's too late.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has access to reliable health information and tools, enabling early detection and prevention of diabetes through technology and education. We envision a future where health disparities are reduced through accessible technology.

How It Works

Our tool helps people quickly assess their risk of diabetes based on simple health inputs like age, BMI, glucose levels, and family history. The assessment provides personalized insights and recommendations for maintaining optimal health.
